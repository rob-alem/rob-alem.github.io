About Me

Dit ben ik.

My name is Rob van Boxtel, born and raised in a village near Zaltbommel and Kerkdriel, named Alem. I am currently 22 years old and fond of following all kind of sports (despite not being a sporty person). Currently I am up to speed about all things regarding football, cycling and a bit of basketball. But by far my favorite sport is Formula 1, which I've been following intensively for over 10 years. I've visited 4 Grands Prix so far; the first being in 2009 in Germany and the most recent was this year in Canada. I am already having plans to visit the race in Austria next year. Other things I like to do in the pastime are gaming, making music with my flugelhorn or having a beer with friends in the weekend. I also used to like playing football at the local football club V.V. Alem, but decided to quit 2 years ago due to some problems with my knee. Since then I joined the coaching staff of the team I was playing in. I play flugelhorn at the local orchestra Fanfare Alem, where I am also responsible for maintaining the archive with sheet music.

I have studied IT for the past 4 years at Avans University of Applied Sciences in 's-Hertogenbosch. After this period of studying (including two internships) I felt ready for my first full-time job. I was specifically looking for a job in my neighborhood and that's how I came across BakkerElkhuizen. I am excited about this new chapter in my life at BakkerElkhuizen and I look forward to the future!
